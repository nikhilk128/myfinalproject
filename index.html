About The Project


This project sets out to conduct analysis on marks which are provided by university on their website. The objective of the project is to extract out useful information which may be used by a number different types of users like admins which require marks to analyze the overall result of a particular institution or college faculties which takes this information for getting an idea about their class students or a particular department. This aims at:

Making students aware and creating a sense of competitiveness amongst.
Making faculties aware about their class and sections as in how many students failed or how many backlogs, etc.
Making HODs aware as to how their department is performing.
Making college directors aware as to where the college stands.
And finally making university admins aware about their university performances.
